The Guitar is the best instrument ever created because:

  1. They are incredibly versitile
    • Guitar can create cords like a bagpipe while also playing single notes like a trumpet.
    • Guitar's variety of sounds allow them to be found in every genre from country music
      in the 1930s to heavy metal in the 1980s.

  2. They are conveinent to hold and handle
    • Unlike a piano, guitars are easily transported.
    • While playing the guitar, you can do anything from lean back in a rocking chair to
      playing while standing up. Other instruments require strict positions to play in.

  3. They are easily modified and advanced
    • Over the course of centuries, the piano is nearly identical now compared to the time it was invented.
      Unlike the piano, guitar's look and sound has changed signifigantly in a matter of decades.
    • The sound and look of the guitar has changed so much because it is a relatively simple instrument.
      Inventing a new sound or shape for an instrument is easier for a guitar than any other instrument.


The Guitar is one of the most popular instruments in the world
